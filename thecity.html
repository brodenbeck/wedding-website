About Rockford

Rockford, Illinois is the third largest city in Illinois after Chicago and Aurora. Settled primarily by Germans and Swedes, it is home to objectively some of the best food and people ever (see: Michelle Williams, Rick Nielsen, Steve Stadleman, Britta Rodenbeck). To get in the Rockford spirit, watch "A League of their Own," eat some Mrs. Fishers potato chips, listen to some Cheap Trick, and hang out with that sock monkey you got a decade ago.

Restaurants & Breweries

During your stay, we encourage you to try some of our favorite local restaurants!

BEEF-A-ROO

6593 Lexus Drive
Rockford, Illinois 61108
(815) 229-6600

If you're in the mood for a quick, American meal, don't want to pay a ton of money, and don't want to go to McDonald's, try Beef-a-Roo. It's like a Portillos, offering burgers, sandwiches, salads, soups, and--their specialty--shakes and malts.

CARLYLE BREWING COMPANY

215 East State Street
Rockford, Illinois 61104
(815) 963-2739

Have you been wondering where a lot of these engagement pictures were taken? Are you downtown for City Market? Do you enjoy craft beer? Go to Carlyle. This place is a staple in Britta and Luke's diets while in Illinois, and it should become a staple for you, too.

MARY'S MARKET

2636 McFarland Road
Rockford, Illinois 61107
(815) 986-3300

Using fresh ingredients, Mary's Market offers a gourment dining experience in an affordable way. From their sandwiches to their soups to their bakery, Mary's is perfect for any meal and any picky eater.

OCTANE INTERLOUNGE

124 North Main Street
Rockford, Illinois 61101
(815) 965-4012

Offering craft cocktails, unique desserts, and moderately priced grub, Octane is a fun place to hang out and have a drink, sip some coffee, or have a meal. Make sure to check their hours, as their kitchen closes for a couple hours between lunch and dinner. Drinks always available.

PIG MINDS BREWING

2080 Steele Drive
Machesney Park, Illinois 61115
(779) 423-2147

Pig Minds not only offers their fantastic craft beer, their better claim to fame is being the first all-vegan restaurant in the area. Meat-eaters will love the food here, too, as they offer traditional choices like fries. But be adventurous! Not so adventurous that you order the garlic fries all for yourself.... looking at you, Luke.

ROCKFORD BREWING COMPANY

200 Prairie Street
Rockford, Illinois 61107
(815) 277-9427

RBC is an absolutely beautiful restaurant and bar in Downtown Rockford located in a remodeled warehouse. Looking for drinks? Head downstairs to the Dockside Taproom. Looking for food and drinks? The dining area offers meals for every eater. Reservations recommended.

Things To Do/Events

If you're looking for something to do while you're in the Rockford area on Labor Day weekend, check out some of these things!

ROCKFORD CITY MARKET

FRIDAY, 3:30-8:30

Downtown Rockford
Water Street & Market Street 61107

Located in Downtown Rockford, the city market is an awesome conglomeration of delicious food trucks/tents, stellar craft beer, and a farmer's market. The market happens rain or shine, as a good portion of it is under a pavillion. This should be first and foremost on your minds for entertainment and dinner if you are in the area on Friday. You might even catch us there if you come before five!

EDWARD'S APPLE ORCHARD

7061 Centerville Road
Poplar Grove, Illinois 61065
(815) 765-2234

Home to the best cinnamon sugar donuts in the entire world. Imagine the best apple orchard ever and multiply it by a thousand, and you get Edward's. Go to the basement of the barn for fresh cider, donuts, and pie, then head upstairs to the store for ample food, home decor, and more.

MCEACHRAN HOMESTEAD WINERY

1917 Wyman School Road
Caledonia, Illinois 61011
(815) 978-5120

McEachran grows its own fruit and grapes for making their own wines year-round. Wine tastings are offered for only $2, and you can walk around the gorgeous grounds. With 150 years of history at this farm, the Homestead is a great place to visit on a warm summer day.

ROCK CUT STATE PARK

7223 West Lane Road
Loves Park, Illinois 61111
(815) 855-3311

If you're looking for some fun in the sun that doesn't involve wearing a swimsuit and hanging out with a bunch of kids in a pool, check out Rock Cut State Park. The park offers hiking trails, canoe and paddleboat rentals, and a beach, all in one convenient location just a few miles from the hotel district. Admission to the park is free, rentals are low-cost, and fun is ensured.

Coffee

Instead of heading to Starbucks for your morning, afternoon, or midnight coffee, try a local brewer!

MEG'S DAILY GRIND

3885 North Perryville Road
Rockford, Illinois 61111
(815) 639-0909

Meg's Daily Grind is a family-owned and operated gourmet coffee shop. They are a specialty coffee shop serving gourmet coffee, espresso, pastries, light lunches, desserts, cold drinks including fruit smoothies and blended ice coffees and teas and chai tea.

KATIE'S CUP

502 Seventh Street
Rockford, Illinois 61104
(815) 986-0628

Katie's Cup is a nonprofit coffee shop located in trendy Midtown Rockford. The shop is well-known for being one of the greenest buildings of the Rockford area. They also feature local arts and play host to a library of books and board games.

ROCKFORD ROASTING CO.

206 North Main Street
Rockford, Illinois 61104
(815) 290-9591

The new Rockford Roasting Company in the heart of Downtown Rockford is both locally owned and operated. The Roasting Company is closed on Sundays, so get your brew on during the week or on Saturday before our event.

Shopping

Are you looking to do some shopping? Did you forget to pack something in your bags? Here are a list of some of our favorite places to shop in the Rockford area.

CHERRYVALE MALL

7200 Harrison Avenue
Rockford, Illinois 61112
(815) 332-2440

Home to over one-hundred stores including Barnes and Noble, Macy's, Bath and Body Works, and about seven nail spas. CherryVale Mall is the largest mall in the area.

CRIMSON RIDGE

735 North Perryville Road
Rockford, Illinois 61107
(815) 399-9000

Crimson Ridge is a boutique store that houses a floral shop in the basement and brands such as Kate Spade, Swarovski, and Vera Bradley. You can find anything from clothing to jewelry to homewares at Crimson Ridge.

CULTURE SHOCK

2239 Charles Street
Rockford, Illinois 61104
(815) 229-2997

Established in 2006, Culture Shock was started by couple Skyler and Lauren Davis. If you're looking for some funky gifts and knick knacks, vinyl records, or unique clothing, head out to Culture Shock.

WOODMAN'S FOODS

3155 McFarland Road
Rockford, Illinois 61114
(815) 986-0220

Open 24 hours a day, Woodman's is your local source for groceries, cosmetics, alcohol, and other miscellaneous items, all on a budget. Plus, Chase used to work here, so that's pretty swell.

FEED 'EM SOUP

251 West Lincoln Highway
DeKalb, Illinois 60115
(815) 310-0903

Britta's pick is Feed 'Em Soup, located in DeKalb, Illinois, where Luke and Britta attended college. While in Alpha Phi Omega, their service fraternity, Luke and Britta both volunteered with Feed 'Em Soup regularly. The organization serves fresh food to the community in a restaurant-style environment to anyone--regardless of situation, background, or ability to pay. Donations help to purchase food and supplies or pay utilities, rent, insurance, and other expenses. Donations are accepted on their website.

EXTRA LIFE - CHILDREN'S MIRACLE NETWORK HOSPITALS

For the past two years, Luke has participated in an event called Extra Life, powered by Twitch. The event consists of playing video games for donations supporting Children's Miracle Network Hospitals. By donating to Luke's campaign, you are helping children across the country gain access to medical care, doctors gain research funding, and parents gain a sense of hope. Donations are accepted on the website. To watch Luke play, check his personal Twitch account. Ask him when he will be online!

TAILS HUMANE SOCIETY

2250 Barber Greene Road
DeKalb, Illinois 60115
(815) 758-2457

Koshka's pick is Tails Humane Society, located in DeKalb, Illinois. Koshka was adopted from Tails in the summer of 2013 as a kitten. Tails is a no-kill shelter, housing cats, dogs, and other small pets while offering rescue, adoption, and spay/neuter services. Tails cares for more than 2,000 displaced pets each year through their in-house services and their foster program. Donations help to purchase food and toys for animals in addition to funding their utilities, rent, insurance, and other program expenses. Donations are accepted on their website.

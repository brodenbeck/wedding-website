Groomsmen

Sam was the responsible roommate at Luke's townhouse, "The Vatican." He is an operations systems analyst at a manufacturing firm and is in school, working towards his Master's in Management Information Systems. Sam dislikes college textbooks, bad budgets, standing still, and seedless watermelons.

John and Luke have known each other since high school, where Luke once duct taped him to a chair. Somehow they've remained friends. He is currently a rental manager at Penske Truck Leasing in Green Bay, Wisconsin. He loves playing golf, long walks on the beach, and watching the Cubs win the World Series.

"Matthew," or Zabel, was Luke's PBFF (Pacemaker Best Friend Forever). Zabel is studying math education at Northern Illinois University, following his dream of being an educator. Zabel's favorite hobby is napping. He has a strong dislike of birds and days that he can't nap.
